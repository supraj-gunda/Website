This was 11th grade, in the middle of January. The construction company was digging up the pipes at the street corner. There was no drinkable water so Dad got a couple of huge water-cooler jugs of water. I think this might have happened while we were moving, because I remember going through the street from our old house to the bus stop. I remember I had to put down the jugs every 20 seconds because they were that heavy and awkward to carry. Keep in mind, it was bitter cold.

Once we got back to the house, I went upstairs and wrapped myself up in bed. It was so cozy and warm. But I was texting someone. And my hands had to be outside the blanket in order to text. This completely froze my hands. Yet, I bore through it and continued texting. Eventually, I got some of the best or worst news I'd ever gotten. I don't remember the exact timeline any more, but it was definitely earth-shattering. My face and chest got very hot.

It was a very odd night of hot and cold.

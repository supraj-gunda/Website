This was the middle of quarantine. I think the end of 9th grade or the beginning of 10th grade. I was on a treadmill in the middle room. I had finished running for 200 calories. I sat down on the white sofa with pink flowers in the back of the living room. I was sweaty but I felt proud. Next to me was the window with light shining through. Perhaps birds were chirping. In front of me was the TV. I don't know if it was on. Mom was in the kitchen. Not sure where Vipin and Dad were.

In that moment, with the sun shining through the window and hitting the rug, I told myself, "I'm going to remember this moment."
And I still do.

This was some night during freshman year of college. It was Shane, Jack, Mikhail, Trakker, Jay, and I. With Mikhail, I was still getting used to the idea of him being a permanent part of the group. I think I was still generally cold to him then. We hated Jay by this point I'm almost sure.

There was an event at Campus Club where we made DIY lanterns. We simply put fairly lights inside a mason jar. Somehow, that night was the foggiest night at Princeton any of us had ever seen. By the time we got to Whitman, we were basically running around playing tag or some sort of horseplay using the lanterns as guides in the fog. It was incredible to see someone across the entire field simply through candlelight. Very cottage core.

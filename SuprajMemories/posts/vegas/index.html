My first trip to California. The summer of 2024 (after freshman year of college). All I can remember is that it was 100+ degrees out side. Every time you stepped out of a building, it felt like a heat wave. The buildings were all very golden. Paradise was right. It felt like New York but if it was all golden, airy, dry, open, spread-out, and in the middle of the desert. I feel like you could see the edges of the city with your bare eyes. It felt comforting that it stopped at a certain point. It felt very rich. We were just about to go over the elevator to the glass bridges that cross the streets. This specific scene was next to a sort of shopping plaza or a strip mall. It was close to the Wynn, near the building with the huge balloon dog statues.
